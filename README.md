 In modern educational institutions, efficient transportation is essential for student 
attendance, faculty mobility, and overall campus operations. Traditional bus management often 
relies on manual processes—such as paper-based bookings, verbal scheduling, and ad-hoc 
announcements—which lead to inefficiencies like overbooking, missed pickups, and revenue 
loss from unoptimized routes. The College Bus Management System (CBMS) addresses these 
pain points by digitizing the entire workflow into a secure, scalable web application. 
 
                     CBMS is a web-based platform for efficient college campus transportation. It 
automates bus scheduling, route management, and seat bookings, reducing manual errors and 
enhancing accessibility.
